About Me

Originally from Muskegon, Michigan, Scott Moore is a graduate of DePaul University in Chicago. After receiving a Bachelor's of Music in Clarinet Performance, Mr. Moore moved to Durham, North Carolina, and established a successful clarinet and saxophone studio. He currently teaches 40-50 middle and high school students each week. Several students have moved onto advanced studies at some of the country's finest music schools.

Having established a successful private studio and performing career in central North Carolina, Mr. Moore is now setting his sights as a web developer. Currently, he is attending the UNC Coding Boot Camp to hone his skills as a full-stack developer.
